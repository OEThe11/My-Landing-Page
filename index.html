About

I am an accomplished Android Engineer and aspiring Project or Product Manager, holding a Bachelor's degree in Biology from the University at Albany, State University of New York, obtained in 2017. My journey into software development began with a Harvard CS50 class project, which ignited my passion for Android development. This pivotal moment, combined with my lifelong fascination with computers, led me to pursue a career in technology, despite initially being unaware of the field of computer science during my college years.

While at Albany, I volunteered at Sure Medical, gaining valuable experience and developing a strong work ethic. This background, along with my technical skills in programming languages such as Kotlin and Java, has enabled me to successfully lead and execute numerous Android projects, delivering high-quality applications that enhance user engagement and satisfaction.

My ambition to transition into a full-time Project or Product Management role has become increasingly prominent as I recognize the importance of strategic oversight and collaboration in achieving product success. I am eager to leverage my technical expertise and project management skills to develop innovative solutions and lead cross-functional teams. My goal is to integrate AI-driven innovations into product development, enhancing both functionality and user experience. I am excited about the future and look forward to making a meaningful impact in the tech industry, whether in a Project Manager role, Product Manager role or as a Software Engineer.
